# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in this project should remain respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of acceptable behavior:
- Giving actionable technical feedback.
- Assuming good intent while challenging weak arguments.
- Focusing criticism on code and decisions, not people.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participation for conduct that harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

Open a private security-style report through GitHub, or contact the maintainers directly through the repository owner account.
